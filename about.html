Our Journey

We hope you love our natural & handcrafted soap recipes & enjoy trying them out sometime. We are a part of MaroofSha's team. MaroofSha is a microscale family organization handcrafting food, personal care products & custom made dresses. All our products are handcrafted with special care with organic additives from local gardens.
